Directions to our Lab

Our lab is located in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Pennsylvania:

3401-C Walnut Street, Suite 300, C Wing, Philadelphia, PA 19104-6228

Parking

There are various parking options around our lab. We recommend street parking. Additionally, there are parking garages near our office. Please note that you are responsible for covering the costs of parking.

Get to our Lab

1 / 6
The outside entrance to our building. It is in between the CVS and Modern Eye.
2 / 6
Take the elevator from first floor to the third floor. The stairs do not directly connect to the third floor.
3 / 6
Once you exit the elevator, enter through the white door on the right.
4 / 6
The entrance to the Linguistics Department is on the left.
5 / 6
The waiting room is through the first door on the left.
6 / 6
Take a seat on one of the chairs. The experimenter will grab you once they are ready.
