Lab location

3401-C Walnut Street, Suite 300C
Rooms 336C & 337C
Philadelphia, PA 19104

Getting here & parking

To get here by SEPTA, you can go to the trolley stop at 36th and Sansom or the MFL stop at 34th and Market.

It is possible, although sometimes difficult, to park on the street near the lab. There are also several parking garages nearby.
